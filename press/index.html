Description

Fistful of Squid is the nom de plume of Joe Trewin, an independent games developer.

Joe has been making and playing games since Mario first squared up against his simian foe. This fascination with games has been present throughout his life providing an outlet for both technical and artistic creativity. By day he works as the lead software engineer at Can Factory, a UK-based technology design company that also supports his game development endeavours. Other hours are spent making games. Previously he's released QB1-0, an arcade space shooter for iOS. Currently he's working on Spooky Pooky, a 2D platformer destined for release sometime in 2015.
